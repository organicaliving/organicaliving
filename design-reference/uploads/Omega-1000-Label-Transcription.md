## 3. RIGHT PANEL: PRODUCT DESCRIPTION & QUALITY ASSURANCE

### Product Narrative
#### Full-Spectrum Omega-3 Complex
Our advanced Omega-3 formula delivers a potent 1000mg dose of essential fatty acids in a single softgel, precision-blended to provide a high concentration of 520mg EPA and 330mg DHA. This full-spectrum profile acts as a powerful anti-inflammatory supplement designed to support cardiovascular health, cognitive function (memory & focus), and vision. We have enhanced this formula with 200 IU of Vitamin E (D-alpha tocopherol) to preserve freshness and provide additional antioxidant support.

Organica is committed to purity and ethical sourcing. We utilize only Halal-permitted fish species with scales—such as salmon, sardines, and mackerel—strictly excluding non-Halal shellfish and bottom feeders. To ensure a pleasant daily experience, our clear, long-shape softgels are infused with a natural Lemon flavor, effectively eliminating fishy burps and aftertaste.

### Storage & Usage Guidelines
Made in an FDA registered and cGMP certified facility. Store in a cool, dry place after opening. Keep out of reach of children. Do not exceed stated dose. Do not use if safety seal is damaged or missing. Color and appearance of product contents may vary. Color shift is normal and does not affect product quality.

Food supplements should not be used as a substitute for a balanced diet and healthy lifestyle.
