## 3. RIGHT PANEL: PRODUCT DESCRIPTION & QUALITY ASSURANCE

### Product Narrative
#### Vegan Pregnancy Support Gummies
Our Pregnancy Support is a precision-blended, comprehensive formula designed to nurture both mother and developing fetus through every stage of pregnancy. We utilize L-5-Methyltetrahydrofolate, the bioactive form of Folic Acid, combined with Choline and a high concentration of Omega-3 DHA (260mg) to support critical neural tube, brain, and eye development. This robust profile also includes 25mg of Iron and Vitamins B6 & B12 to combat pregnancy-related fatigue and support healthy red blood cell formation.

Organica understands that pregnancy requires uncompromising quality delivered in a format you can actually stomach. We have successfully engineered a high-potency profile—including typically hard-to-flavor nutrients like Iron and Omega 3—into a delicious Strawberry Flavored Gummy. This ensures you receive your daily requirement of 26 essential nutrients, including CoQ10 and Calcium Malate, in just one enjoyable gummy per day, without the stress of swallowing large pills.

We are proud that our Vegan Pregnancy Support Gummies are Certified Vegan, Halal, non-GMO, and Gelatin-Free.
